# PolySignal

> Premium intelligence for prediction markets.

PolySignal is a modern intelligence platform that analyzes prediction markets by tracking elite trader behavior, market consensus, and conviction trends.

Rather than simply displaying market data, PolySignal transforms raw information into actionable intelligence that helps users discover and evaluate high-conviction opportunities.

---

## Vision

Prediction markets contain an enormous amount of information.

PolySignal exists to answer the questions that matter:

- Where is smart money moving?
- Which opportunities deserve attention?
- Why is this signal important?
- Which traders are driving conviction?
- What changed recently?

Every feature is designed to improve decision making through explainable market intelligence.

PolySignal is designed around a simple philosophy:

- Consistency over novelty.
